# shopify-app-store-analysis
Dashboard: Shopify App Store Analysis
Reporting Period: Latest available data
Key Insight: Across 500 apps, the total amount of reviews has steadily increased from 2018 to 2024. Total reviews are at 7.98k and an average rating of 4.19.
Business Impact: The growth of the business is clearly shown, as total reviews, reviews year-to-date, and reviews per year have steadily increased, reflecting strong business health.
Recommendation: Trends show the business is strong. Focusing on reviews and customer satisfaction is critical to the success of the business. With a current average rating of 4.19, the business is strong, but shows room for improvement. Focusing on what we can improve to get the average reviews closer to a 5.0 is the best course of action.
